Stop by the House

Our house is located on Fraternity Row, 831 Castle Point Terrace. We are right between Phi Sigma Sigma and Delta Tau Delta. Please feel free to stop by and say hello! There is normally a brother around. Come hang out to relax or use our Academic library for school. Whatever the reason, don't be stranger!

Come to our Informationals

Our Informationals are held several times during each semester so be sure to check out facebook and our flyers for dates! This presentation is for you to learn about our fraternity and the brothers on campus and this presentation is open to anyone, so bring whoever you like!

Stop by our Events

Bored and need something to do? Well come and check out our events and we'll make sure you have a good time! Our events range from academic tutoring or planning to a 3 hour dance lesson in Jacobus! Want to be a better professional? Or do some community service? Or just forget about school? Whatever it may be please stay tuned and stop by!

Explore Campus

Make sure to be on the look out around campus for a LUL brother, as we are heavily invovled around campus. From WCPR to Orientation Leader, we are pretty much everywhere. Don't be surprised to catch us in Student Life Booking rooms or talking to faculty, we tend to keep ourselves busy. So please reach out and come help us bring new things to campus!

La Unidad Latina, Lambad Upsilon Fraternity Inc. is a Latino based national Greek Lettered Organization. We have been at the forefront of educational issues for men of color since 1982.
